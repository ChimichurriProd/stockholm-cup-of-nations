How it works

The format.

Fast, non-stop 7-a-side football at Östermalms IP — 25-minute matches, group stage on Saturday, knockout all the way to the final on Sunday. Max 16 players per squad. Free entry for spectators.

Matchday 01 · Saturday 25 July

Group stage

All 32 nations in eight groups of four. The top two in every group advance to Sunday's knockout.

About us

More than
a tournament.

Organiser Stockholm United Sport Academy

Welcome to a tournament where community, diversity and the joy of football take centre stage. Stockholm Cup of Nations is more than a football tournament — it's a tribute to Stockholm's unique multicultural spirit.

Created by Stockholm United Sport Academy and founded in October 2022, our vision is to unite cultures and nations through the universal language of football — a prestigious annual cup where players with roots from all over the world compete under their national flags.
